I love Borscht

Especially when someone cooked it for me

Borscht is the national dish of Ukrainians, a symbol of Ukrainian culinary culture and prosperity. It is the main first course of Ukrainian cuisine. There are three main varieties of borscht in Ukraine: red borscht, green borscht and cold borscht. The most common type of borscht is red borscht, which is cooked from chopped beets, cabbage with the addition of potatoes and various seasonings. The common feature of it is probably a sour taste. Borscht gained international recognition.
